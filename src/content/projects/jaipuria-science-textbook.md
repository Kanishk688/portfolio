A full-book illustration project for a Grade-4 science textbook — one of the first
AI-illustrated textbooks in the region. The work spans **13 chapters**: chapter
openers, classroom and outdoor scenes, science diagrams (the solar system, the
digestive system, forces and energy), and two recurring hero characters who guide
students through the book.

Every illustration was built in a single consistent style — bright, clean,
fully-saturated colour with crisp editorial shapes — then upscaled and prepared
at 300&nbsp;DPI for print.
