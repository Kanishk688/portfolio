A full Grade-3 science textbook, illustrated end to end — chapter by chapter,
from the human body and healthy food to plants, animals, weather, soil, birds
and the night sky.

Each chapter blends three kinds of artwork: **warm opener scenes** that draw
children in, **clear teaching diagrams** (the water cycle, photosynthesis, the
solar system, soil layers) that make ideas easy to grasp, and **friendly
everyday moments** they can relate to. The style is bright, consistent and
child-first, then prepared at print resolution.
